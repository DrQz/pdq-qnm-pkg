Book PPA 1998' refers to "The Practical Performance Analyst" book originally published in 1998.
Usually abbreviated as PPA.

There are 2 editions. 
"The Practical Performance Analyst: Performance-By-Design Techniques for
Distributed Systems" (McGraw-Hill 1998). Second Printing (iUniverse 2000). See
http://www.perfdynamics.com/iBook/ppa.html for details.

Each sub-directory corresponds to the relevant chapter in the PPA book and
all of those PDQ models are written in the C language.
